This notice records the MPL-2.0-covered WebGAL and WebGAL Terre work used by the Haneoka story editor. It supplements, and does not replace, the upstream license texts retained as [`LICENSE.webgal.txt`](LICENSE.webgal.txt) and [`LICENSE.webgal-terre.txt`](LICENSE.webgal-terre.txt).

## Audited upstream revisions

- [OpenWebGAL/WebGAL](https://github.com/OpenWebGAL/WebGAL) at `e7f0abeb855b5b442460743bdaa9778ca751b43f`;
- [OpenWebGAL/WebGAL_Terre](https://github.com/OpenWebGAL/WebGAL_Terre) at `7b7a2159a5ccead80327437b7305b8fdb47a4e5f`.

These revisions are the snapshots against which the current Haneoka files were audited. They are not presented as the unknowable first-copy date of every adapted line. Both retained upstream `LICENSE` files are byte-identical MPL-2.0 texts with SHA-256 `1f256ecad192880510e84ad60474eab7589218784b9a50bc7ceee34c2b91f1d5`; the files remain separately named so that the provenance of both projects is not lost.

Relevant Terre history includes the resource-view commits `ba3540a742e6efe4f0af7d31c076c13715f2c3cc` and `fd9ae6d5122383f8622b386a81579d1607ee0b31`, Add Sentence/Ribbon commits `8be1d57b504584504de0e08bd9461d4fe94d06d9` and `baa9bde38bfb72413be4988ea3dd5d60ce0a876f`, and virtual graphical-list commit `ca3de36c99b2f1dcc9e6c3ce7b0fe99e009a9bda`.
